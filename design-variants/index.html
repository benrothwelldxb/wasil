Wasil Design System

Parent App Design Variants

6 distinct design directions for the Capacitor-delivered parent dashboard. Each focuses on native mobile usability with personalisation. Click any card to view the full mockup.

Option 2
iOS Native
Follows Apple HIG closely. Large title navigation, frosted glass headers, system-grouped lists, and native interaction patterns. Burgundy as tint colour.
SF Pro-inspired Backdrop blur Grouped lists Large titles
Option 3
Material You
Google Material Design 3 with dynamic colour generated from burgundy. Tonal surfaces, rounded shapes, pill-shaped nav indicators, and M3 components.
M3 Components Tonal palette 28px radius Surface tint
Option 4
Warm & Approachable
Child-centric with per-child daily cards. Soft rose/coral palette, generous touch targets, friendly emoji, and a "Headspace meets school" feel.
Child-centric Warm palette Friendly UX 48px+ targets
Option 5
Clean & Minimal
Swiss-inspired, typography-driven design. Serif/sans-serif pairing, generous whitespace, colour-coded timeline, and information density through hierarchy.
Typography-first Serif pairing Timeline layout Whitespace
Option 6
Bold & Modern Dark
Dark mode with vibrant accent colours and glow effects. High contrast, bold typography, and a premium tech-forward aesthetic.
Dark mode Glow effects High contrast Tech-forward
Option 7
Editorial & Premium
Magazine-inspired with a "daily briefing" metaphor. Playfair Display serif, newspaper-style layout, two-column grids, and editorial typography.
Editorial layout Serif typography Newspaper grid Premium feel
