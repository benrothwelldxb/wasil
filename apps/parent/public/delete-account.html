Delete Your Account

VHPS CoA Connect — powered by Wasil Connect

We're sorry to see you go. This page explains how to request deletion of your account and what data is affected.

How to Request Account Deletion

Option 1: Contact your school

Ask your school administrator to delete your account from the admin panel. They can remove your account immediately under Parents → Registered Parents → Delete.

Option 2: Email us directly

Send an email to privacy@wasilconnect.com with:

  • Your full name
  • The email address associated with your account
  • Your child's school name
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"

We will process your request within 7 business days.

What Data Is Deleted

When your account is deleted, the following data is permanently removed:

  • Your account profile (name, email, password)
  • Parent-student links (your association with your children)
  • Direct messages and conversations with staff
  • Form responses and survey answers
  • Event RSVPs
  • Attendance requests (absence reports, pickup requests)
  • ECA activity selections and preferences
  • Consultation bookings
  • Service registrations
  • Notification preferences and device tokens
  • Message acknowledgments and read receipts

What Data Is Retained

The following data may be retained for school records and regulatory compliance:

  • Audit logs referencing your actions — retained for up to 1 year, then automatically deleted. These logs record administrative actions (not message content).
  • Your child's school records (student name, class, attendance records marked by staff) — these belong to the school and are not deleted when a parent account is removed.

Important Notes

  • Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • If you need to access the app again in the future, your school will need to send you a new invitation.
  • Deleting your account does not remove your child from the school's records.

For questions about your data, contact privacy@wasilconnect.com
